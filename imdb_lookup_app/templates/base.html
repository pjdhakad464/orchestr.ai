IMDb Dataset Utility

Bulk `nm` / `tt` to Name Lookup

Convert IMDb ids into titles or people, reverse-match exact normalized titles and person names back to IMDb ids, and export the result as CSV or Excel.

Modes

  • `auto` tries id, title, and person matching
  • `id_to_name` resolves `tt...` and `nm...` directly
  • `title_to_id` finds matching title ids
  • `person_to_id` finds matching person ids
