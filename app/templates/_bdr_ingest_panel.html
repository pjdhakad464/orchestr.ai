construction BDR Ingest Builder

Build an Ingest-Ready BDR

Upload a BDR workbook and a change list. Changes are applied and colour-coded (green added · red removed · yellow updated · blue new row), every change carries a DataOps comment, twitter_search_terms is rebuilt per the DAR rule, and a Legend sheet with the session summary is added. Research-required fields (IMDb, Wikipedia, unprovided handles) are flagged, never invented.

Change-list format

Pipe syntax is the most reliable (title is matched with or without the - DAR suffix):

  • ADD | <title> | instagram | somehandle
  • REMOVE | <title> | tiktok
  • RENAME | <old title> | <new title>
  • NEW | <Name> | Talent | gender=Woman; type=Actress,Producer; instagram=handle; twitter=<url>

Plain-English lines (e.g. “Instagram: <url> for <title>”, “remove the TikTok from <title>”, “rename X as Y”) are also parsed. Anything ambiguous is recorded in the Legend sheet rather than guessed.
